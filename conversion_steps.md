Conversions happen in the following order of priority:

1. When in hyperspace, convert volatiles to fuel up to the fleet's fuel usage and up to the largest of 80% fuel capacity or fuel capacity - 500 units. Do not use the last 15 volatiles.
2. Convert metals to supplies up to fleet's supplies usage plus the sum of one combat deployment of each military ship.
3. If metal not available, convert transplutonics to supplies up to fleet's supplies usage plus the sum of one combat deployment of each military ship.
4. In either normal or hyperspace, convert volatiles to fuel up to the largest of 80% fuel capacity or fuel capacity - 500 units regardless of usage. Fuel not used in normal space. Do not use the last 15 volatiles.
5. Convert all metals to supplies. 30/100 = 0.3. Less is better for compression.
6. Convert all ore to metal. 10/30 = ~0.33.
7. Convert all transplutonic ore to transplutonics. 75/200 = 0.375.
8. Convert all organics to domestic goods. 30/50 = 0.6. Luxury goods not producible aboard ships for balance and lore aspects.

Lower priority conversions only happen when there's surplus capacity from higher priority points.
